Common Myths vs Facts

Explore common misconceptions about Australia’s history and culture and the actual facts.

Myth: Indigenous Australians had no technology

Fact: Indigenous Australians had advanced tools, fire management, and complex trade systems.

Myth: Australia was empty before Europeans arrived

Fact: Over 250 language groups existed, with established communities, laws, and sustainable land practices.

Myth: Indigenous culture is static

Fact: Indigenous cultures are dynamic, evolving, and continue to thrive today.
